CHRONICLES & ACHIEVEMENTS

2025

Moroccan Day of Mathematics (MDM)

The Math & Science Club – INPT took home the Best Stand Award at Euromed University of Fès. Exploring the "Invariance Principle," the team stood out among 1000 enthusiasts on April 19th.

2025

Cyber Odyssey – AKASEC CTF

Ranked 13th in the student division of Morocco’s biggest cybersecurity competition on October 18th. NEXUS_MSC proved its strength, finishing just two challenges shy of the national finals.

2026

IDEH CTF 7th Edition

NEXUS_MSC continued its momentum, securing an impressive 5th place globally in the International Days of Ethical Hacking organized by CIT INPT.

PRESENT

MSC Cicada – The Challenge

The hunt is on. A series of cryptic puzzles and hidden layers inspired by the legendary Cicada 3301. Running throughout Ramadan, this intellectual odyssey tests the limits of the brightest minds.
